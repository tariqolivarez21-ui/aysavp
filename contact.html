Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book your services?

We recommend booking 4-8 weeks in advance, especially for larger events. However, we can often accommodate last-minute requests depending on availability.

Do you provide travel to events outside Atlanta?

Yes, we can travel for events within the greater Atlanta region and surrounding areas. Contact us for events further out.

Can you help with custom audio-visual design?

Absolutely! We work with you during the consultation to design a custom AV solution that fits your event and budget.

What if something goes wrong during my event?

Our team will be on-site during your event with backup equipment and solutions to handle any issues that arise.

Do you offer rental-only without setup?

Yes, we can rent equipment for you to set up yourselves, but we recommend professional installation for best results.

What is your cancellation policy?

Please contact us directly to discuss our cancellation policy for your specific event.
